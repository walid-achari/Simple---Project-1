Leonardo DiCaprio

American Actor

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio (/dɪˈkæprioʊ/, Italian: [diˈkaːprjo]; born November 11, 1974) is an American actor, film producer, and environmentalist.

  • 1974 - Born in Los Angeles.

  • 1979 - In 1979, DiCaprio was removed, at the age of five,

  • 1996 - In 1996, DiCaprio appeared opposite Claire Danes in Baz Luhrmann's film Romeo + Juliet,

  • 1997 - In 1997, DiCaprio starred in James Cameron's Titanic (1997) as twenty-year-old Jack Dawson,
    a penniless Wisconsin man who wins two tickets for the third-class on the ill-fated RMS Titanic.
